# Alora 

> Your life, organized beautifully.

Alora is a cozy digital notebook that helps you keep track of your daily health, workouts, habits, and personal thoughts all in one single place. 

It is designed to look like a beautiful paper scrapbook with playful sticker badges, custom colors, and handwriting fonts!

---

#  Cool Features We Built

##  Scrapbook Journal
- **Movable Sticky Notes**: Click a button to add random colorful note scraps. You can drag and drop them anywhere on your screen!
- **Click-to-Write Pages**: Click straight inside any sticky note to write down quick thoughts.
- **Tactile Notebook Paper**: A big, clean center page with horizontal notebook lines that match your text handwriting perfectly.
- **Mood Stamp**: Choose how you feel today using a quick smile selector menu.

##  Daily Agenda Planner
- **Hour-by-Hour Timeline**: Put your tasks right next to the exact hour of the day you want to do them.
- **Playful Category Stickers**: Tag your daily plans with cute colored sticker labels like Gym PR, Wellness, or Nutrition.
- **Focus Mode Filters**: Click a single sticker chip to hide everything else and only look at your fitness tasks or food tasks.
- **Paper Background Studio**: Click colorful circle buttons to instantly swap the color of your planner page (Warm Cream, Retro Rose, Mint Balance, or Legal Yellow).
- **Washi-Tape Mantra Box**: A bright, easy-to-read white text box held down by virtual tape to write your daily motivation words.

##  Health & Wellness
- **Water Hydration Log**: Click a simple glass button (+8 oz) to fill up a bright blue loading progress bar.
- **Daily Meal Tracker**: Type in what you ate for breakfast, lunch, or dinner, and tag it as Healthy, Neutral, or Indulgent.
- **Hormone & Cycle Rhythm**: Tap symptom badge chips like Bloated, Fatigued, or Energetic to see how your body shifts across a 28-day cycle map.

##  Personal Preference Studio
- **Focus Toggles**: Tell Alora if your main target is fixing hormones, muscle hypertrophy, or just staying organized.
- **Weight Targets**: Change your scale weight numbers with simple plus and minus clicks.

##  User Documentation Handbook 
- **Interactive Reading Menu**: A clean side selector layout that lets users click through sections smoothly to learn how the product functions.
- **Feature Deep Dives**: Explains step-by-step instructions for things like dragging the sticky notes, filtering the planner hours, or cycle logging.
- **Technical Specifications Manual**: Includes a comprehensive code blueprint for each section written in simple words so anyone can read it easily.

#  Our Tech Stack

## Frontend (The parts you can see)
- **React.js**: The core building engine for our page views.
- **Material UI (MUI)**: For our clean buttons, inputs, and popups.
- **Yarn**: Our lightning-fast package manager assistant.

## The App Layout (How it is styled)
- **Floating Sticky Navbar**: A blur-glass top menu bar that stays pinned to the top of your screen when you scroll.
- **MainLayout Shield**: A smart layout shield that adds 75px of padding to the top of your pages so they never hide behind your floating navbar.
