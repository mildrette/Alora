# Alora

> Your life, organized beautifully.

Alora is an all-in-one wellness, productivity, and self-improvement platform designed to help users track, organize, and improve their daily lives.

The platform combines health tracking, journaling, fitness management, habit building, and productivity tools into one personalized experience.

---

# Features

## Health & Wellness
- Meal tracking
- Nutrition insights
- Water intake tracking
- Weight tracking
- Sleep tracking
- Hormonal and cycle tracking
- Mood tracking

---

## Fitness
- Workout logging
- Gym progress tracking
- Exercise history
- Fitness goals
- Body progress monitoring

---

## Productivity
- Daily planner
- Task management
- Habit tracker
- Reminders
- Calendar organization

---

## Journaling
- Personal diary
- Mood journaling
- Reflection prompts
- Emotional tracking

---

## Personalization
- Custom dashboards
- Personalized goals
- Theme customization
- User-specific recommendations
